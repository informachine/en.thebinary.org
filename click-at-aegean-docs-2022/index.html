Click at Aegean Docs 2022

The Binary Newsroom Published on September 20, 2022

Friday 30 September, at 9.30 pm at the Municipal Theater of Mitilini, the non-profit organisation The Binary presents its short film: "Click, a story about digital rights" directed by Yiannis Christoforou.

The film was chosen to be screened by the 10th Aegean Docs Festival and presents, in an understandable way, the necessity of recognising the digital rights of citizens, many of whom (as you will see in Binary's video) do not know why they concern them.

Click is a film on the story of digital rights. In short, it's you in a film.

It starts with human existence and carries you into the jarring effect of the digital world, reaching to the point where you are called to make the decision about the future. All these on a journey full of light that reveals the "invisible" and the "intangible", in a digital world that is absolutely real, between stars and beautiful screens, between mathematics and emotions, between everything you have already lived and what shapes your future, between you and your mirror.

This is a story about the anthropocentric nature of technology that takes you by the hand for a quick ride through a journey you have never been through, with the brightest and sometimes the darkest views of the technological cosmogony. 

A journey into the other side of the "Wonderland", where everything runs faster than life. Where everything changes in a “click".
