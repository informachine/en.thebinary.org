A Voter's Guide and 7 Tips to Detox Your Data, in partnership with Tactical Tech

The Binary Newsroom

Voter’s Guide is a set of resources designed to promote citizens’ critical thinking about the use of their personal data by political groups to influence public opinion, elections, and other political participation.

 

Across the world, data on citizens is being collected, traded and utilised to influence their political views . Despite increased attention in recent years, it is still difficult to access information about the industry actors or the technologies that they use to sway citizens' political views. This project aims to increase accessibility and understanding to strengthen civic capacity to engage with politics.

 

In partnership with Tactical Tech, the Voter’s Guide pack contents are based on the extensive research conducted by “Data and Politics Team” with partners from over 20 countries on how technology and personal data is used to influence political opinion.
